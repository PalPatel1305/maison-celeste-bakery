Our Story

Crafted with
love & patience

✦

At Maison Céleste, every loaf, croissant, and pastry begins long before sunrise. We use stone-milled heritage flours, wild-caught sourdough cultures, and seasonal ingredients sourced directly from Ontario farms.

Our recipes draw from three generations of French boulangerie tradition — adapted, refined, and baked fresh every morning with the care of a family kitchen and the discipline of a professional kitchen.

How We Bake

From starter to shelf

🌾
Source
Stone-milled heritage flours and seasonal produce sourced from Ontario farms weekly.
🥣
Mix & Ferment
Long cold ferments of 24–72 hours develop deep flavour and natural digestibility.
🔥
Bake
Deck ovens with steam injection, starting at 5 AM, every single morning without exception.
🛍️
Fresh to You
On the shelf by 7 AM. What doesn't sell is donated to local shelters by close of day.
